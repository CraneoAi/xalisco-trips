Mazamitla Tour from Guadalajara

Magical Town · Pine Forests · Waterfalls · Zip Lines · Cool Mountain Air

What makes this tour special: Mazamitla is where Guadalajara locals escape the heat — a charming mountain Magical Town at 2,200 meters elevation with cobblestone streets, wooden balconies, pine forests and waterfalls. Completely different from every other destination in Jalisco. Bring a light jacket — it's refreshingly cool up here.
