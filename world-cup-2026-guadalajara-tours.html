Chapala + Tonalá Combo
8-10 hours • $50 USD per person

Experience Mexico's largest lake at Chapala, then explore the artisan heart at Tonalá. Visit local markets, museums, and enjoy authentic regional gastronomy.

Tequila + Guachimontones
8-10 hours • $55 USD per person

Combine tequila and pre-Hispanic history. Visit agave distilleries, then explore unique circular pyramids of the Teuchitlán culture at an interactive archaeological site.

  • UNESCO + Archaeological combo
  • Museum with artifacts included
  • Panoramic valley views
