We depart early — around 7:30 AM — to arrive at Guachimontones before 10:00 AM. This is the best time to explore the archaeological site before the heat and large crowds arrive. The reward is worth the early start.

Arriving before 10:00 AM is critical. The archaeological zone heats up significantly after mid-morning, and between 11 AM and 3 PM it's virtually impossible due to extreme heat. Plus, there are fewer visitors early on, allowing you to enjoy the experience without crowds. Guachimontones is waiting cool and fresh.
