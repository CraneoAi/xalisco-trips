We depart at 8:30 AM, arriving in Tonalá around 9:15 AM. This is the perfect time to explore the artisan market when it's fresh and artisans are actively working in their workshops.

We start in Tonalá because the artisan market and workshops are most active in the morning — you get better access to the artisans, fresher inventory, and cooler temperatures for walking. Then we head to Chapala in the early afternoon when the lakefront is most beautiful and relaxed.

In Tonalá you'll find:
  • Artisan market with ceramics, blown glass and textiles
  • Live artisan workshops
  • Masks Museum (unique exhibition of traditional Mexican masks)
  • Local shops and galleries
  • Authentic restaurants and cafés

Chapala, Mexico's largest lake, offers:
  • Waterfront promenade walk
  • Historic center with colonial architecture
  • Local market (fresh food, crafts)
  • Panoramic lake views
  • Local restaurants (fresh fish especially)
